<h2 style="color: #222222; font-size: 28px; font-weight: bold; margin-top: 25px;">What Makes Quantum Computing Different</h2>
<h3 style="color: #222222; font-size: 22px; font-weight: bold; margin-top: 15px;">Why Quantum Systems Are More Powerful Than Regular Computers</h3>

<p>Traditional computers process information using bits that exist as either 0 or 1. Quantum computing works differently by using quantum bits, also called qubits, which can exist in multiple states at the same time.</p>

<p>This allows quantum systems to process huge amounts of information much faster than standard computers. Problems that could take normal systems years may eventually be solved within minutes using advanced quantum technology.</p>
